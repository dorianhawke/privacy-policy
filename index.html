Introduction

DH Games ("we", "our", "us") operates the ORO mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information from our users.

ORO is a completely offline game that:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not collect any user data
  • Does not store any personal information
  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools

Data Storage

The only data stored on your device is:

  • Your high score (stored locally on your device)
  • Game settings and preferences (stored locally on your device)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children under the age of 13.

Third-Party Services

ORO does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
